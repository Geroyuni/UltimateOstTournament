# The Ultimate Game OST Voting Tournament
Find the best song of your favorite games by pitting them against each other. You will be shown two songs at a time, and you pick which one you like most. The songs that you picked compete against each other in another round up until one song is left victorious. Play alone or share your screen with friends!

This site contains a selection of 2048 game songs handpicked for quality by a group of people over the duration of 2 years.

### Play at https://geroyuni.github.io/UltimateOstTournament
